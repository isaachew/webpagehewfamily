Hi,I am Isaac,and I am 8 years, 6 months old because my birthdate was on 22nd October 2008. I love coding; I code Python and HTML. I even coded this webpage myself! One of my highlights is the AIMS orchestra camp, held at ISP.

On Friday the 5th of May 2017, my mother dropped me at Garden International School at the Recital Hall, at 7:15 to meet Mr Koelma. The bus left at 7:45. I sat with Rock, my orchestra partner. When we reached ISP (International School @ Parkcity), we were led to the Multi Purpose Hall (MPH). There were children from other schools: Alice Smith, ISP, our school, and Nexus International School. Then we went up to the rooms one floor up for Sectionals, where we practiced by section. I was Violin 2, but allocated to Violin 1, which means that the some of the pieces said "Grade 4/5". We played Burung Kakak Tua and Pirates of the Caribbean (violin 3).

As I went to the MPH, I felt proud because I was going to play in an orchestra! I put my case and bottle beside my chair for the rehearsal. After that, we played Short Ride in a Fast Machine and The Firebird (Violin 2). At lunch, I ate deep-fried fish. I also ate that the next day. After that, we played the remaining ones: Overture (violin 2), In the Hall of the Mountain King (violin 2) and Highlights from Harry Potter (also Violin 2). Then I went home with my mummy. The concert was on Friday 6th of May 2017, my sister's birthday.
